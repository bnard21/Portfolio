¿Qué onda?

I’m a software developer and artist who enjoys building digital products where creativity and technology meet. My background in art shapes the way I approach development — I care just as much about how something feels and looks as I do about how it works.

I like creating web and mobile experiences that are intuitive to use but still visually distinct. To me, good software should feel intentional — clean, thoughtful, and designed with people in mind.

A lot of my work is about turning ideas into something real. Whether it’s solving bugs, refining features, or shaping the overall design of a product, I enjoy the process of taking something rough and transforming it into something polished and functional.
